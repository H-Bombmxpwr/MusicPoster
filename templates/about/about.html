What is Music Poster?

Music Poster is an open-source project designed to bring album art to life! Using Spotify's Web API, this tool generates visually stunning posters for your favorite albums and artists.

How It Works

Music Poster uses Spotify's API to fetch album details, including the album art, tracklist, and metadata. Once an album is selected, the tool analyzes the album art to extract dominant colors, ensuring the generated poster is visually cohesive. Users can further customize the poster by adjusting background and text colors.

The posters are created dynamically using Python's Pillow library, which enables high-quality image rendering. Finally, the generated posters are displayed on the website, ready for download or sharing!
