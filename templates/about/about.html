Album Poster

What is Album Poster?

Album Poster is an open-source tool that transforms your favorite music into stunning visual art. Using Spotify's Web API, it automatically generates beautiful, print-ready posters featuring album artwork, tracklists, and perfectly harmonized colors.

Whether you're decorating your space, creating gifts for music lovers, or just exploring creative design, Album Poster makes it effortless to celebrate the albums that matter to you.

Key Features

  • Automatically generate posters using high-quality Spotify album art
  • Customize background and text colors to match your style
  • Smart color extraction analyzes artwork for perfect harmonies
  • Search by artist, album name, or paste a Spotify URL directly
  • Full tracklist display with optional formatting styles
  • Surprise Me feature generates random posters from trending albums
  • Download high-resolution PNGs ready for printing or sharing
  • Fully responsive design works beautifully on any device

How It Works

When you search for an album, Album Poster connects to Spotify's API to fetch comprehensive album data including high-resolution artwork, complete tracklists, artist information, and release details.

The tool then analyzes the album cover using advanced color extraction algorithms to identify dominant colors. These colors are presented as suggestions, ensuring your poster maintains visual harmony with the original artwork while giving you full creative control.

Posters are rendered dynamically using Python's Pillow library, which enables precise typography, layout composition, and high-quality image output. The result is a professional-grade poster you can download instantly.
